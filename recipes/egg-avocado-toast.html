Egg Avocado Toast

Descriptions

Egg avocado toast is a simple and nutritious dish made with ripe avocado, poached or fried eggs, and toasted bread, often garnished with salt, pepper, and other toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice sourdough bread, toasted
  • 1/2 cup mashed Haas avocado
  • 1 large hard boiled egg
  • 1 teaspoon Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Toast the slice of sourdough bread until golden brown.
  2. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread.
  3. Slice the hard boiled egg and place it on top of the avocado.
  4. Sprinkle Everything Bagel Seasoning over the egg and avocado.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
