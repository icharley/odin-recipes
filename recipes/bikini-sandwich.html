Bikini Sandwich

Description

A bikini sandwich is a delicious grilled sandwich made with ham, cheese, and sometimes mustard, typically served hot and crispy.

Ingredients

  • 4 (1/2 inch thick) slices heaty white bread, such as Farmhouse
  • 2 ounces thinly sliced serrano ham
  • 4 ounces Manchego cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter, softened
  • Optional: mustard or mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, on the skillet.
  4. Add the ham and cheese on top of the bread.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from heat, slice in half, and serve hot.
